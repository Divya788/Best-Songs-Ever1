Shawn Mendes


Facts

  1. He can play both the piano and the guitar.

  2. Shawn Mendes is the youngest artist since Justin Bieber to have a Number One album!

  3. Shawn can only speak English fluently, however, he does know some Spanish & Portuguese.

  4. He is Canadian, NOT American.

  5. He started his career on the social media app, Vine, singing small parts of popular songs.

Songs


There's Nothing Holding Me Back



In this song, Shawn Mendes appears to be head over heels in love with a lady and he is willing to do all she wants. He later revealed that the 'lady' was actually a fictional character he really liked.

In My Blood


A common theme in most of his music is his relationships, but this time, he’s singing about the lack of a friend and his fear of being alone. It’s incredibly personal and he shows a lot of emotion.
