Marshmello


Facts

  1. Marshmello first gained notoriety in the Electronic Dance Music community by releasing remixes of songs by Jack U and Zedd.

  2. Marshmello is said to be one of the fastest growing DJs in the world!

Songs


Happier




“Happier” is a breakup song by Marshmello and Bastille. The lyrics of this song tell the story of a love affair between the narrator and his significant other that is rapidly coming to its end.

Alone


‘Alone’ is Chris’s most significant record since reinventing to Marshmello. Lyrically, the song is about missing home and the person you love.
