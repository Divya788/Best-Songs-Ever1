Guess The Song

Welcome! Here you will play a fun music based game!


Directions

  1. Listen to the snip of the song

  2. Guess what the song’s name is, and the singer

  3. Write down your guesses with the song number

  4. Compare your guesses with the answers at the bottom

The songs on here are also featured on the ‘Artists’ page! If you listen to those, you have a better chance of getting them right. To easily get to the Artists page after you have guesses the song, click on the artists name in the answers.
