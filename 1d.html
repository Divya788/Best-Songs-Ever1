One Direction


Facts

  1. Zayn Malik, Liam Payne, Harry Styles, Niall Horan, and Louis Tomlinson joined together in 2010 all the way until 2015

  2. Zayn Malik left the group in 2015, so the band went on hiatus. Harry Styles became very popular after their break up.

  3. They are an English-Irish pop boy band.

  4. They all joined together on The X Factor and Simon Cowell revealed that it “took him 10 minutes to put them [One Direction] together as a group.”

  5. Niall Horan's natural hair color is brown. He has been dying it blond for years.
