Finish The Lyrics

Welcome! Here you will play a fun game where you try to finish the lyrics.


Directions

  1. Listen to the snip of the song

  2. Try to guess what the rest of the lyrics are

  3. Hover over the 'Answer' button and you'll see the answer

  4. Be careful NOT to hover over the 'Answer' button before steps 1 and 2

If you click on the song's name, it will take you to the Artists page who sang this song. Also, I have not included the song name before the snip of the song, because it might give hints to what the rest of the lyrics are!
